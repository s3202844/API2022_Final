Audio to MIDI

MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) was proposed in the early 1980s to solve the communication problem between electro-acoustic instruments. MIDI is the most extensive music standard format in the arranger world, and it can be called "music notation that computers can understand." It uses digital control signals of notes to record music [1].

Considering the importance of this part, we chose a novel research, Basic Pitch, as the basis of our work [2]. This research was released in conjunction with Spotify's publication at ICASSP 2022. It introduces a lightweight yet powerful Automatic Music Transcription (AMT) model. To train our model, we use three datasets, MTG-QBH, MAESTRO, and MUSDB18 [3-5].
